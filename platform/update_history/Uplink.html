Changes/Additions

  • In the event of a crash, system information and your email address will be sent to Introversion for debugging purposes, with the your consent
  • Saved games from this version will be incompatible with older versions of Uplink
Bug fixes
  • Fixed custom theme not loading
  • Fixed a potential graphic crash occuring on agent load and when seeing the dialling box
  • Fixed crash problems related to invalid native resolution (ex: 800x600 is not a native resolution on LCD)
  • Fixed crashes with some irregular command line options
