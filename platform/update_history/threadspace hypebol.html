[+] July 19, 2007
Changes/Additions
  • ·Forced Team Balance has been enabled by default. Server ops can still disable this feature (not recommended). When on, players will be sorted into teams using a smart algorithm based on skill level of their account (not their character). Note that once you have been placed on a team, you can still leave and switch with someone – if someone is willing to do the same. Or you can observe and wait for another person to fill that other lopsided slot if you wish.
  • ·Training is now a Team Elimination type game, with faster resurface time after being destroyed.
  • ·In Official Training servers (only), a bot will join to fill a slot if any team imbalance occurs. He will leave once the teams are balanced again (as long as a teammate is alive).
  • The Refer-A-Friend feature has been implemented. See the http://Starport.Hyperbol.com for promotional details. Two new unique ship skins were created as part of the promotion – they are not recolors of existing skins. They use shiny / glossy shaders, too. Good luck!
  • Official Deathmatch servers now MUST choose a specific skill bracket. In other words, you may no longer host an ‘open’ deathmatch server, but instead, must pick a skill bracket. Available brackets are novice or Pro.
  • Trainee skill rating is reserved for the Training gametype only.
  • Several maps have had their surface beacon and surface locations changed to better support flow and capture.
  • Surface beacons now take less time to capture, and some have a larger capture radius.
  • Laser turret in mining facility can no longer be repaired once destroyed.
  • Production Station Modules now build about 3x faster!
  • 5v6, 6v7, and 7v8 is now allowed in team games, without stopping the extra person from being able to play.
  • Ship Kills are still awarded on the basis of who did the most damage, but now it only counts recent damage – about a 30 second window before damage dealt by a target is cleared.
  • You no longer stop moving / cancel your move, if you run into friendly repulsors, attractors, and repair drones. You bounce off them and continue going.
Bug fixes
  • Some minor memory leaks found and fixed during map loading
  • Some minor memory leaks found and fixed during console command execution
  • Destroying a turret or other destroyable object should no longer result in brief lag.
  • Fixed a very rare crash on game load (at about 3% loading, in the loading screen)
  • Auto-downloading of custom maps from servers properly configured now works. Please see the wiki at http://wiki.hyperbol.com for additional details.
