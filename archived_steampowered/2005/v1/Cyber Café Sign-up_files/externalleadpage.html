Next Steps

After you click the submit button, a Quote form will be displayed on your screen. If you would like to proceed in becoming a member of Valve's Cyber Cafe Program, please print the Quote form, fill out the bottom section, and fax to 425-952-2784. Please allow up to 2 business days for us to contact you after you have faxed the order to us. We will e-mail your license keys and installation link for the software. If you have any questions in the meantime, please feel free to get in touch with us.

Adding your café to our database will require you to agree to the terms of our Steam Subscriber Agreement. To view this agreement, please visit: http://www.steampowered.com/index.php?area=subscriber_agreement.
