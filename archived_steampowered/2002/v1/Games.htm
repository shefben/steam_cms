Deathmatch Classic breathes new life in to the Deathmatch experience. It features rejuvenated graphics into the Half-Life engine, with updated weapon models and animation. Day of Defeat
Day of Defeat is a World War II multiplayer game which recreates WWII battles in both fictional and non-fictional environments. DoD puts you in the shoes (rather, combat boots!) of an Allied or Axis soldier circa 1944, Western Europe.

Its class-based system lets you play different types of soldiers - from Rifleman to Machine gunners to Snipers. Each class has its own mix of special abilities and weapons. With 10 maps and a selection of nearly 20 weapons, players will have something new to try every time they play.
