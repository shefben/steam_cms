BETA TEST STEAM

If you are interested in Beta Testing Steam, please complete the following steps:

     1.  Download and Run the Steam Installer:
          http://www.simplyhealth.com/setup.exe
          http://mirrors.cstrike.org/mirrors/valve/steam/setup.exe
          ftp://grid.valvesoftware.com/setup.exe
          http://www.computergames.ro/download/steam/setup.exe
     2.  Click Here , choose "Open" to run key generator or "Save" to download it, then start the key generator.
     3.  Enter your email address for the "account ID" and hit enter to receive your account creation key.
     4.  Please refer to the Step-by-Step section of the support site for account setup instructions.
     5.  Have a good time beta testing Steam.   

During the beta test, if you have any problems please email: steamsupport@steampowered.com or visit the Steam Support Site

*Steam requires a Broadband connection. Term “broadband” refers to any Internet connection capable of sustained throughput of at least 256Kbps. We have designed it to deliver the optimum experience at 384Kbps. The technology will work at lower speeds but the experience will be slower and may require longer waiting periods before content is available. The only forms of phone line transmission omitted by this restriction is ISDN (128Kbps) and traditional dial-up modems (<56Kbps).
