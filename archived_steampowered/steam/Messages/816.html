Friday, November 3 2006


This past week we played host to a number of editors from the hardware press to share our plan for taking advantage of multicore CPUs in the next few months. Bit-Tech and ExtremeTech have posted their recap of the event so far, and there should be stories showing up at other outlets over the next few weeks.
The Ship is currently running a free weekend, where you can play the full game from now until this Sunday at 1:00 p.m. PDT. There are thousands of people playing as part of the trial right now, so click here to find out if this is the game for you.
The holidays also appear to have come early here at Valve, where the team at Taito has delivered to us our very own Half-Life 2: Survivor arcade machine. Most of us are enjoying the game immensely. But earlier today, Robin Walker took a break from working on Team Fortress 2 to play HL2 Survivor -- his game ended almost immediately, and he blamed his crushing defeat on the game's lack of a mouse and keyboard. We just thought you should know.
