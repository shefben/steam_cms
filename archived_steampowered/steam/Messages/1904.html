Friday, October 17 2008




PC Gamer has given Far Cry 2 an "Editor's Choice" rating in their upcoming holiday issue. Their reviewer says "Far Cry 2 hasn’t just exceeded my expectations, it’s blown them away." It's available for pre-purchase now in the Steam store for a $5 discount.
Left 4 Dead, Valve's new zombie apocalypse simulator, is available for pre-purchase at a 10% discount. We think the game is seriously awesome. Yes, we're a little biased, so maybe you should take our advice with a grain of salt, but if you're going to do that you should also take it with a mouthful of delicious human brains. And then go ahead and pre-purchase the game immediately, because you've been infected with an irresistible desire for more. You don't want to miss out on the co-op, competitive, and single player zombiefest come release day.
