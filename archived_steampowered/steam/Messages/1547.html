Friday, April 25 2008


The folks over at GSC have been busy with their upcoming title S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Clear Sky, releasing a high-definition tech demo movie showing off the game. Steam will be the exclusive digital download platform for S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Clear Sky, the anticipated prequel to the award winning S.T.A.L.K.E.R.

Team Fortress 2 continues to expand. The latest in the Team Fortress 2 shorts, Meet the Scout, is available on Steam now. A major update for TF2 is due next week, adding an all new map, Goldrush, and new achievement-based weapons for the Medic. Pick up TF2 here and get ready.
If frenetic is your thing, check out Bullet Candy, the arcade-style shooter, which is now only $3.95. This game was built by a one-man shop--pick it up today and Charlie will thank you.
