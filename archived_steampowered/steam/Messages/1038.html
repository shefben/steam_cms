Friday, May 11 2007


This past week, PCGamer UK and CVG (Computer and Video Games) ran a few stories on our upcoming games. They had nice things to say about their initial impressions of Half-Life 2: Episode Two, Portal, and Team Fortress 2. They also wrote a piece on their trip to Valve, which touches on some upcoming changes to Steam.



Also, beginning today, new and existing owners of The Ship will automatically receive one Guest Pass, which lets you invite a friend to play the game for 3 days, free of charge. Please note: if a friend invites you to try The Ship, it's not because they want to share a friendly cruise - it's because they want to sneak up on you in the ship's bathroom & knife you in cold blood.
