Friday, April 13 2006



Seeing a new community emerge around an upcoming game is always pretty exciting for us. A couple of weeks ago we hosted the guys behind Left 4 Dead's first fansite, Left 4 Dead 411. They've since posted their first installment of two huge articles from their experience playing the game.

Don't forget to hand out your guest passes to friends if you're a Counter-Strike: Source owner. To see if you have a guest pass available just look next to the game's name in the Steam games list. The recipient of the guest pass doesn't need to have Steam installed; you can send the pass by entering an e-mail address and it'll guide them though the process.
