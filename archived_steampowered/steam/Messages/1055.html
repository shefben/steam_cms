Friday, May 25 2007


This week IGN ran a multi day feature covering all of the games coming in the Orange box this fall. There are stories devoted to Episode Two, Team Fortress 2, and Portal that have a bunch of new information about the games in them. Definitely a must-read if you're planning on picking up the box.



In Source MOD news, it looks like the Insurgency team is getting close to releasing the first version of their game. From the beginning, this looked like one of the more interesting and ambitious MODs in development, and the screenshots look absolutely stunning. They are planning on shipping sometime next month, head over to their site to tell them to hurry up.
