Friday, June 8 2007


If you're going to be in the San Jose area next Saturday, you can come by the Showdown LAN event to hear Gabe talk a bit about what we've been up to, get a free copy of Half-Life 2, and most importantly, play Left 4 Dead. This will be the first time we've had this game playable in public, so it should be a lot of fun. You can get more info about the event from their website.
In other news, we've recently released (with the help of a number of community volunteers from all over the world) a new support site which is translated into 21 different languages so far.
If flying off mile-high ramps, shooting out over office buildings, and navigating towering loops at break-neck speed is your thing, you'll want to check out Track Mania United. It comes out on Steam on Thursday June 14, and you can preorder the game now for 20% off the release price.
