Friday, January 5 2007


Next week, we will release a Steam client update with several new features. The biggest addition will be Guest Passes -- a new feature that lets you give your friends free trial access to your favorite multiplayer games. Also in the update are a streamlined auto-updating mechanism for the Steam client itself, an "add to favorites" feature for the games list, and a new look for the user interface.

For a more complete description of these new features, you can get the full rundown here.
  

A couple of Mods caught our eye recently. Rock 24 is a single-player Half-Life 2 mission in which you (as Gordon Freeman) awake in a prison holding cell and have to rescue a scientist. Pirates, Vikings and Knights II has entered beta, with a download available on their website. Try it out and play as this barrel-chested hairy beast.
Oh yeah, by the way: Happy New Year!
