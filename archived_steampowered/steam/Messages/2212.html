Friday, January 30 2008




School buses with nitro rockets now on Steam. Check out Monster Trucks Nitro, the latest game from Red Lynx, the makers of Trials 2. This video says it all.
There is also a new trailer showing off some of the multiplayer functionality in the much anticipated Empire: Total War from Sega and Creative Assembly.
Steam now accepts retail keys from Unreal Tournament 3. There’s a massive update for Unreal Tournament 3 on the way from Epic and you can get ready for it by either buying UT3 or activating a product key you already have. UT3 will be updated automatically via Steam and introduce support for Steamworks Achievements (with over 50 Achievements), plus a host of new features and enhancements in AI, Server Browsing, Demo Recording, Mod Support, and more.
Lastly, we opened up our official YouTube channel. Right now it features the TF2 Meet the Team shorts. We’ll continue to post more content up; take a look and see what you think.
