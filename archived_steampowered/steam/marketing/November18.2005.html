Friday, November 18 2005


The releases we have coming over the next few weeks are piling up, which means that over the next month we're going to be very busy but the communty is going to get a lot of new content. In between trying to get all of the work done for the Day of Defeat: Source and Counter-Strike: Source releases we have planned, around Valve we've been playing a lot of Darwinia. We play virtually every game that comes out, and it's rare for everyone to be universally excited about one in the way that have with Darwinia. We're looking forward to putting it in front of people December 15th on Steam.
We've spent some time on getting some new products into the Valve Store. Specifically, we made a T-Shirt that you can wear if you ever feel like you need to control a mechanical dog, along with a limited edition lithograph of the Citadel from Half-Life 2. Some of the new items are in very limited quantities, and are likley to sell out quickly.
And while not Steam related, Half-Life 2 for the XBOX is out in stores now. If you'd like to see what Half-Life 2 looks like in your living room, or you haven't played Half-Life 2 on the PC yet, head down to your local retailer and check it out.
