Friday, December 16 2005


We have one more week of releases coming to Steam before we close out 2005. Next week there will be two new player models, one for each team, released for Counter-Strike: Source. Early next year we'll be releasing a completely rebuilt version of cs_militia. There is also a fairly high chance we'll be releasing an update to Day of Defeat: Source next week, which will include a number of small changes.



Earlier this week Darwinia was launched, and from what we've gathered, people are really enjoying the game. If you're not sure if Darwinia is for you, check out the demo here.

We'd also like to congratulate the folks over at Introversion Software for Darwinia's 4 nominations at next year's Independent Games Festival. As we've mentioned a number of times, we're all huge fans of the game, and it's great to see the game get the recognition it deserves.
