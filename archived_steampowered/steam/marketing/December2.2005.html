Friday, December 2 2005


We've been having fun the past few days playing on Internet Day of Defeat: Source servers on the new map "Argentan," and on Counter-Strike: Source's updated version of de_nuke. So far both maps have been really well received - we're glad everyone is enjoying them as much as we are. Next up on the Counter-Strike: Source front will be the release of two new player models.

This week on Steam, Darwinia became available for pre-purchase at a discount of 10% off the full price, $19.95 USD. (This discount will remain in effect until the game is released on December 14th.) We're really excited to ship this title. Hint: don't make too many plans for December 15th, because you'll be tired from staying up all night. Here's a video preview of Darwinia in action (52MB).

The folks over at Ritual also have been keeping the community up to date with the development of the Sin Episodes. There are a couple of in-game screenshots below, and more over at their site.
