Legal Agreement

HALF-LIFE 2: DEATHMATCH MAP MAKING CONTEST OFFICIAL RULES

1. ELIGIBILITY: Contest is open to legal residents of countries where not prohibited by law, who are 13 years of age or older as of the date of entry. Employees of Valve Corporation and their immediate family members are not eligible.

2. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS: All submissions must be original works created specifically for this contest. Maps must be functional Half-Life 2: Deathmatch levels that compile without errors.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: By submitting an entry, contestants grant Valve Corporation a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, and distribute the submitted content.

4. JUDGING: Entries will be judged by Valve employees based on creativity, gameplay, technical execution, and overall quality. Judging decisions are final.

5. PRIZES:
• First Place: $5,000 USD cash prize
• Second Place: $3,000 USD cash prize
• Third Place: $3,000 USD cash prize
Winning maps may be included in future Half-Life 2: Deathmatch updates.

6. SUBMISSION DEADLINE: All entries must be received by 11:59 PM PST on [CONTEST END DATE].

7. GENERAL CONDITIONS: Valve reserves the right to disqualify any entry that violates these rules or contains inappropriate content. Winners will be notified via email and must respond within 30 days to claim prizes.

Final Submission

Once you click "Submit Entry", your contest submission will be final and cannot be modified.
