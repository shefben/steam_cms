Action Half-Life
  

About the Game

Action Half Life is designed to simulate the experience of being in an Action movie, and aims for something called 'Movie Realism.' This means that you can take a few bullets and keep fighting. You can fall from a two story roof and only hurt your leg. You can bandage a wound in seconds.
