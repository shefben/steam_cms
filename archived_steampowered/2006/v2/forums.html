Before posting a problem in the forums, please use the new interactive Steam Support system.. It contains answers for many of the most common problems and questions.

Use the Steam Support system.

Below are some forum usage guidelines. They are posted here as a general reminder in an effort to keep the forums friendly and usable for everyone.

  • The forums are for everyone, new and advanced user alike.
  • Before you post a question, use the forum search feature to determine whether your topic has already been covered.
  • Do not start flame wars. If you have a problem with someone, message that person and carry on a private discussion. Also, if someone has engaged in behavior that is a detriment to the message board -- spamming, flaming people, etc -- contact one of the forum moderators. Flaming the offensive user will only increase the problem.
  • Please post in the correct forum.
  • Administrators/Moderators reserve the right to change, edit, or delete any content at any time if they feel it is inappropriate.

The bottom line is: respect others and you will be treated with respect. Be rude and disrespectful, and you'll not find much help here.

I agree to these terms.
