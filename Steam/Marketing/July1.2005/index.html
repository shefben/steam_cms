Friday, July 1 2005


Next week we're planning on releasing the remade version of cs_assault to Counter-Strike: Source owners. If you don't currently own CS: Source, you can purchase it through Steam via any of the Half-Life 2 packages. Along with the release of cs_assault, we'll be including a rebuilt version of the existing Terrorist model. Over the next few months we'll be expanding the model selections for both teams.



If you're a MOD developer, or just someone who likes to keep an eye on the MOD community, head on over to the Valve Developer Community that we launched earlier this week. The site is based around a Wiki, which is a webpage that allows the community to contribute to the site directly.
