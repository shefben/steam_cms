SOURCE ENGINE BETA TEST

As we get closer to releasing Half-Life® 2, we need to deploy the Source engine to a wide group to find any remaining configuration issues. We will accomplish this by conducting a limited-time beta test of Counter-Strike™: Source™ via Steam to a select group of our customers. During this beta, problems can be reported using the in-game bug-reporting tool. In addition any crashes will automatically generate a report back to our bug database. Valve thanks you for your on-going support, and we hope that Half-Life 2 lives up to your expectations.
