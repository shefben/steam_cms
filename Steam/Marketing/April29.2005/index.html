Friday, April 29 2005


This week we're going to be talking about some Source-based projects from folks other than Valve.

The team over at Plan of Attack released the first version of their game, which focuses on strategic team game play, where the players on each team are presented decisions at the start of each round that impact the flow of the game. Here at Valve we got a chance to play the game before it was released and had a lot of fun playing with their development team. Congratulations to their entire team for getting it out the door, and we're looking forward to watching it evolve over time. Head on over to their website to download and play the latest version.

Another interesting Source-based game has been announced today. The Alien Swarm team launched a new site showing off the Source version of their game, and IGN has the first preview up on their page. We're all looking forward to seeing what their team can do with their game using Source.

Lastly, we promised some new media on the Counter-Strike: Source version of de_inferno this week, but we didn't quite have enough time to get everything together. We're now shooting for getting that done next week.
