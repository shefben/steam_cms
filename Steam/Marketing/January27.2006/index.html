Friday, January 27 2006


Preloading is underway for two new products on Steam, Space Empires IV and Dangerous Waters. If you'd like to be ready to play those games the moment they are available, you may preload them now. The preload for Red Orchestra, is also right around the corner.

In February we'll be releasing two more Counter-Strike: Source player models. Those will be the Leet Krew and SAS, one for each side in the game. They should be ready for a screenshot next week.

Next week there may also be a challenge match to watch between some people at Valve and the HL2CTF team. If we get all of the details nailed down, we'll make sure everyone knows how to watch it via SourceTV.
