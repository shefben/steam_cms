Friday, May 19th 2006


Episode One is set for release June 1st via Steam and at retail outlets around the world. As we approach the launch of our first episodic release, we will be releasing a series of short gameplay clips that will focus, like Episode One, on Alyx. You will find these shorts in the new My Media area of Steam.

One of the many Source MODs we've been keeping our eye on is Nuclear Dawn, and apparently some of the more popular game publications have taken notice as well. Computer Gaming World and PC Zone have both recently featured the class based post-apocalyptic FPS/RTS hybrid in their magazines, and we wanted to make sure the community had all taken a look at what they are building.

Finally Sin Episodes: Emergence, which shipped to North American retailers and via Steam on May 10th, will be available at retail outlets in Western Europe next week. Check your local stores for more information.
