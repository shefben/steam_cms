Friday, September 19 2008




We've mentioned that Crysis Warhead is available on Steam, but you may not have noticed the new multiplayer experience that's included with Warhead, called Crysis Wars. It's essentially a stand-alone multiplayer game in its own right, which is included when you purchase Crysis Warhead. It's got 7 new maps and plenty of vehicle-based combat. Here at Valve, we're a little too wrapped up with putting the finishing touches on Left 4 Dead to play much of anything else, but we're hearing from customers that Crysis Wars has lots of variety and is definitely worth checking out. There's a brief HD trailer for Crysis Wars on Steam. If you haven't checked out the Crysis Warhead trailer, be sure to give that one a look too.
