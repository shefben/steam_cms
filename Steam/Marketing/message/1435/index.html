Steam News - Friday, February 8 2008


Steam brings you the best in PC gaming. The 11th Annual Interactive Achievement Awards, honored Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare with Overall Game of the Year, Action Game of the Year, and Outstanding Achievement in Online Game Play. And, one of our newest additions to Steam, Puzzle Quest: Challenge of the Warlords, won Downloadable Game of the Year. Bioshock won Outstanding Achievement in Sound Design, Original Music Composition, Story Development, and Art Direction. The Orange Box was honored Computer Game of the Year and Outstanding Achievement in Game Design and, for Portal, Game Play Engineering and Outstanding Character Performance. Congratulations to all the winners.
