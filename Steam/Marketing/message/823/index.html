Friday, November 10 2006


This week on Steam we had the debut of the Unreal 3 powered Roboblitz, a humorous game where you play as a robot (named Blitz) who has to use a number of gadgets and gizmos to get a Space Cannon up and running. We've also seen significant updates to both Dark Messiah and X3 Reunion released.
A new item has been added to the Valve merchandise store for fans of our beloved robot D0G. For about 10 dollars you can get a 28.5" x 16" poster of him squaring off against a Strider in Episode Two. Click here to check it out.

We'll be showing off what the retail box will be looking like on MTV's gaming show G-Hole next Wednesday the 15th at 8:00 p.m. This will include three short videos from Episode Two, Team Fortress 2, and Portal.
