Friday, January 18 2008




ShackNews dropped by Valve’s offices the other week to take an early peek at Left4Dead, the co-op horror survival game slated for release later this year. They also played on Team Fortress 2’s "Badlands," one of two new maps on the way soon.
Monday is the last day to nab the Eidos Holiday Pack, which includes the newly released Kane & Lynch: Dead Men, plus other Eidos Action titles Tomb Raider: Anniversary, Infernal, Just Cause, Project: Snowblind, and Rogue Trooper, for just $89.95. There are also special weekend deals on Painkiller Overdose and a limited pack of UFO: Afterlight and Earth 2160 to check out as well.
